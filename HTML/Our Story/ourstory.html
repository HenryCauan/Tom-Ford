Tom Ford, Life
Journey
2005
Tom Ford launches his own brand, Tom Ford International, after a successful career at Gucci and Yves Saint Laurent. He decides to create a line of luxury perfumes.
2006
The first perfume of the brand, Tom Ford for Men, is released. This fragrance is known for its sensuality and sophistication.
2007
Black Orchid is launched. This perfume becomes one of the brand's most iconic fragrances, featuring notes of black orchid, spices, and citrus fruits.
2011
Tobacco Vanille is released. With notes of tobacco and vanilla, it becomes an instant hit.
2013
Velvet Orchid is launched, offering a softer and more delicate version of Black Orchid.
2018
The Tom Ford Beauty line is introduced, including fragrances like Tom Ford Black Orchid Voile de Fleur.
2020
The brand continues to expand its fragrance line, launching new scents and exclusive collections.
