Tom Ford is synonymous with sophistication, innovation and boldness in the world of fashion and beauty. Recognized as one of the most influential designers of the 21st century, he transformed brands such as Gucci and Yves Saint Laurent before launching his own label in 2005. His line of perfumes reflects his vision of timeless luxury and modern sensuality, combining exclusive ingredients and impeccable design.
